General FAQs

  • What is GradeFlow?

    GradeFlow is the student portal to manage submissions, track progress, and access resources.

  • Who can access GradeFlow?

    All enrolled students with valid credentials can access the portal.

  • What browsers are supported for using GradeFlow?

    Chrome, Firefox, and Edge are recommended. Internet Explorer and older browsers may not be fully supported.

  • Who should I contact if I encounter issues with my account?

    Use the “Contact Support” section on the Help page or email ClearTrack-helpdesk@gmail.com.

Account & Login

  • How do I reset my password?

    Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

  • Can I change my email address?

    Contact support to update your email address in the system.

  • Can I use a personal email instead of a school email?

    For security and verification purposes, GradeFlow requires your official school email for authentication.

  • Do I need to verify my account?

    In some cases, the system may ask for verification through your school email. Follow the instructions provided.

Submission FAQs

  • How do I submit my clearance documents online?

    Log in to GradeFlow, go to "Submit Documents," select the clearance form, upload the required files, and click Submit.

  • Can I sign my clearance form digitally?

    Yes, you can either use the portal’s digital signature feature or upload a scanned signed copy.

  • What happens after I submit my clearance documents?

    Your submission will be reviewed by the designated department. You can track the status in "Submission History."

  • How long does it take for a clearance submission to be approved?

    Approval times vary depending on the department, usually 1–3 business days. You will receive a notification once approved.

  • Can I edit or resubmit my clearance documents?

    Yes, if your submission is still marked "In Progress" or "Pending," you can upload a new version before final approval.

  • What should I do if a document is rejected?

    Check the feedback in "Submission History" and re-upload the corrected file. Contact support if you’re unsure.

  • Are there file size or format restrictions for clearance documents?

    Yes, supported formats are PDF, DOCX, JPG, PNG. Maximum file size is 10MB per document.

Technical FAQs

  • My file upload keeps failing. What should I do?

    Ensure you have a stable internet connection, the file type is supported, and the file size is below 10MB.

  • I cannot digitally sign the clearance form. Any workaround?

    You can print, sign, scan, and upload the form as a PDF.

  • The submission status doesn’t update. Why?

    Refresh the page or check again after a few minutes. If it persists, contact support.

  • Can I use my mobile device to submit documents?

    Yes, the portal is mobile-friendly. Make sure your files are accessible from your device.

  • Why am I seeing an error when uploading a file?

    Make sure the file is in a supported format (PDF, DOCX, JPG, PNG) and does not exceed the 10MB size limit. Clear your browser cache and try again if the problem persists.

  • The portal loads very slowly. What can I do?

    Check your internet connection, close unnecessary browser tabs, or try using a different browser. Chrome, Firefox, and Edge are recommended.

  • I cannot see my submission status update.

    Refresh the page, or log out and back in. If the status still does not update, contact support with your submission details.

  • Digital signatures are not appearing on my document.

    Ensure you are using the latest version of the portal and a supported browser. You can also upload a scanned signed copy as a temporary solution.

  • Some features are not working on my mobile device.

    Make sure your mobile browser is updated. Certain features work best on a desktop or laptop. If issues persist, try switching to a recommended browser.

  • I accidentally uploaded the wrong file. How do I replace it?

    If the submission is still "In Progress" or "Pending," you can delete or replace the file in the portal. Otherwise, contact support for assistance.

  • I'm getting a "Session expired" message frequently. Why?

    This happens if you are inactive for a long time. Log in again and avoid long inactivity periods. Clear cookies if the problem persists.

  • Why does my uploaded document appear blurry or unreadable?

    Check the original file resolution. For scanned documents, use at least 150 dpi. PDF uploads preserve quality better than images.

  • Why can't I download my submitted documents?

    Ensure you are logged in, and that your internet connection is stable. If the download link still does not work, contact support.

  • I get a browser compatibility warning. What should I do?

    Use Chrome, Firefox, or Edge. Older versions of Safari or Internet Explorer may not support all portal features.
